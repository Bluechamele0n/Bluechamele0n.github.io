Destiny, Angel of Fate, Profile

Angel Profile: Destiny, The Angel of Fate

Title: The Angel of Fate, The Weaver of Threads, The Clockbound Watcher
Dominion: Fate, Time, Subtle Guidance
Creator: Fionil, the goddess of time, space, mind and order

Lore
Crafted by the divine hand of Fionil, Destiny exists as her eternal messenger and steward of mortal fate. Unlike other Angels who act through direct intervention, Destiny’s influence is subtle, his presence more felt than seen. He moves like a shadow across the Tapestry of Fate, tugging on threads when necessary to align events with the grand design of Fionil’s will. Mortals may witness his work in moments of coincidence, fortuitous encounters, or unexplainable shifts in fortune. Some call him the "Clockbound Watcher" for the faint ticking sound that often accompanies his presence, said to be the sound of unseen gears turning in his wake.

Destiny played a pivotal role in several of the greatest trials in mortal history, including:

  • The Banishment of the Voidbringer: Through his foresight, he guided the champions to a convergence of fate that allowed them to seal the Voidbringer beyond the Tapestry’s edge.
  • The Rise of the Holy Knights: It was Destiny’s subtle nudges that drew the chosen knights to their fateful callings, leading to the formation of the most honored holy order in the world.
  • The Stopping of the Blightking: He foresaw the fall of the Blightking's fortress and led a warrior and a scholar to find the one path to victory.
  • The Veil’s Shattering and the Repairing of the Tapestry: When reality itself began to fray, Destiny’s hands were the ones that wove it back together, stitch by stitch, his patience and precision saving all from a collapse into chaos.

For these deeds, the gods themselves offered him godhood, yet he declined. "I do not rule. I guide," he said, refusing the crown with quiet resolve. Instead, he returned to his post, weaving fate’s threads in silence, his loyalty to Fionil unyielding.

Personality
Destiny is calm, patient, and endlessly observant. He views time as a flowing river, knowing when to let it drift and when to cast a stone to ripple its course. His voice is often cryptic, laced with riddles that leave mortals guessing. While his words may seem distant, those who listen carefully can discern wisdom hidden within his parables.

He possesses a dry, sarcastic wit, often muttering quips under his breath that only the most perceptive can catch. Despite his aloof nature, his love for Fionil is absolute. He holds her in the highest regard, seeing her as both mother and guide, and he models his patience and restraint after her. While other Angels may act with fiery passion or divine judgment, Destiny’s touch is as light as a feather—just enough to shift fate’s course without disturbing the flow.

Appearance
Destiny’s appearance is as enigmatic as his presence. He manifests as a tall, imposing figure cloaked in elegance and mystery. His form is hidden beneath a flowing, deep purple robe lined with intricate silver embroidery, each thread a symbol of ancient cosmic design. His face is concealed behind a smooth, expressionless mask adorned with flowing black patterns—neither human nor inhuman, simply other. No eyes can be seen, yet all who meet him feel as though they are being quietly observed, their pasts and futures weighed in silence.

A tall black top hat, complete with a single sleek feather, crowns his head, lending him an ageless, theatrical dignity. His massive, raven-black wings fan out in graceful arcs, each feather touched by starlight and shadow, shimmering like galaxies trapped in midnight. Wherever he walks, soft purple feathers drift around him, suspended as if weightless. If they do not fall, they are simply returning to the bottom of his wings—symbols of fate momentarily released and then reclaimed.

There are no exposed gears or cogs, yet time itself seems to bend faintly around him. When he walks, the air hums with a soft, rhythmic ticking—as if the very concept of time follows in his wake, unwilling to move on without him.

Abilities & Powers
Foresight

"To glimpse the future is to carry the burden of restraint."
Destiny’s most iconic power is his ability to see into possible futures. Unlike prophecy, which is fixed, Destiny sees possibilities—potential outcomes that may shift with a single decision. He reveals only glimpses of what might come, preferring to let mortals learn through experience rather than spoil the journey. Those who meet Destiny in dreams or visions often receive cryptic clues, not direct answers.

Thread Manipulation

"I do not break the thread, nor do I bind it. I nudge it."
At the heart of Destiny’s power lies his ability to touch the threads of fate that connect all things. With a simple touch, he can "nudge" these threads, subtly shifting the outcome of a person’s fate. Unlike mortal magic, his influence is nearly imperceptible, but the effects can be monumental. A chance meeting, an unnoticed opportunity, or a moment of courage in the face of despair—these are the results of his guidance.

Chronoshield

"Time is an ocean, and I know when to let it freeze."
When danger closes in and no other recourse remains, Destiny can stop time for a fleeting moment. This power, known as the Chronoshield, halts time within a small radius, giving him precious moments to think, act, or reposition. Unlike mortal time magic, which bends reality, Destiny’s Chronoshield does not shatter time’s laws. Instead, it is a pause—a brief breath between moments, like the stillness at the top of a clock’s pendulum swing. He rarely uses this ability, as even a brief pause in time’s flow can cause ripples further down the line.

Final Destiny

"The time has come, for your final Destiny."
Destiny holds deep respect for free will but is unwavering when someone defies the will of his beloved Fionil while standing at the precipice of ruin. In these rare and dire moments, he offers the individual a final choice—an opportunity to align with fate willingly or face the full force of his divine judgment. This choice is not without consequence, for one path demands acceptance and obedience, while the other leads to erasure from existence itself. Regardless of their decision, the target's Destiny is irrevocably sealed, and the threads of fate are rewoven to ensure balance is maintained. Destiny only invokes this power when all other options have been exhausted, making it both a test of resolve and a grim reminder of his divine authority.

Stat Block (D&D 5e)

  • Name: Destiny, Angel of Fate
  • Race: Angel (Celestial)
  • Alignment: Lawful Neutral
  • Armor Class: 21 (Divine Reflex)
  • Hit Points: 375 (30d10 + 150)
  • Speed: 40 ft., Fly 80 ft.
  • Abilities:
    • STR: 14 (+2)
    • DEX: 22 (+6)
    • CON: 20 (+5)
    • INT: 24 (+7)
    • WIS: 28 (+9)
    • CHA: 26 (+8)
  • Saving Throws: Int +14, Wis +16, Cha +15
  • Skills: Insight +19, Perception +19, Persuasion +15
  • Damage Resistances: Radiant, Necrotic, Psychic
  • Condition Immunities: Charmed, Frightened, Stunned
  • Senses: Truesight 120 ft., Passive Perception 29
  • Languages: All, telepathy 120 ft.

Actions

  • Multiattack. Destiny makes two Fatewoven Blade attacks.
  • Fatewoven Blade. Melee Weapon Attack: +14 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 32 (4d10 + 7) radiant damage.
  • Weave of Fate (Recharge 5-6). Destiny targets a creature within 60 feet. The target must succeed on a DC 23 Wisdom saving throw or be subject to one of the following effects of Destiny’s choice: Their next attack misses automatically, their next saving throw is made at disadvantage, or they lose the ability to take reactions for 1 minute.

Thus is the Watcher of Threads known. Those who glimpse him may never recognize his hand, but know this: when fate’s course shifts and chance feels too perfect to be random, Destiny’s hand has brushed the threads. His patience is infinite. His guidance is subtle. But his influence is undeniable.
